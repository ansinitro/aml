Conclusion

  • Seasonality Matters: SARIMA reduced error by ~5x compared to ARIMA for monthly data.
  • Context Matters: ARIMAX allows incorporating external economic drivers.
  • Interdependence Matters: VAR is essential for systems where variables influence each other.
